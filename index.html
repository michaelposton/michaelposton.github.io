Case Studies

How Digital Desk is helping our customers.

Rates Desk

Global Investment Bank

A world-leading rates desk specialising in sovereign debt , corporate debt and treasury inflation-protected securities needed to increase operational efficiency and scale their resource to meet increasing client demand. Digital Desk provided an online space for clients to receive trade ideas, research, product information and live rates, inside and outside of market hours.

Structured Markets Desk

Global Investment Bank

As part of a digital transformation initiative, a busy Structured Markets desk wanted to modernise their legacy process of emailing PDF documents to clients. Digital Desk provided an online space to market quant products such as synthetic indices , risk premia , and momentum strategies , and publish models and trade ideas on structured notes with equity and rate payoffs, alongside other content, in a secure and compliant manner.
